Solution-1

递归：本质上，将原来的问题，转化为更小的同一问题

举例：数组求和
Sum(arr[0…n-1]) = arr[0] + Sum(arr[1…n-1])
Sum(arr[1…n-1]) = arr[1] + Sum(arr[2…n-1])
…
Sum(arr[n-1…n-1]) = arr[n-1] + Sum([])

Solution-2
